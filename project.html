My Reflection

I picked soccer because I played soccer for 7+ years before I moved to Topeka so soccer is something that is familiar to me. My process for selecting players was to look up a random number generator on google and choose whatever player the number corresponds with. For the forwards I knew what the z-scores for all of the variables were already so I picked the best 3 but from there for the midfielders, defenders, and the goalie were all the first 3 variables. My process was neither math or sports based, it was luck and ease based. From the start knowing it was mostly luck I was going to be happy with a positive team z-score so with a team z-score of 16.66, yes I am happy. No, I didn't change any players during the process and I wouldn’t do it either. No, I didn't look at the statistics much to see if one player's z-score was better than others, so no I wasn’t expecting anything so I wasn’t surprised by anything.
